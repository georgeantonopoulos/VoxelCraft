# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style

All documentation and in-game text should be clear, concise, and technical. This ensures that the information is easy to understand and follow, especially for developers and AI agents working on the project.

## Brand Messaging

The brand messaging for VoxelCraft should revolve around creativity, exploration, and limitless possibilities. The game should inspire players to be creative, to explore the world, and to feel a sense of wonder.

## Visual Identity

The visual identity of VoxelCraft should be guided by the aesthetic of the provided logo. The goal is to create an immersive and beautiful world that feels organic, detailed, and mysterious.

### Logo

The logo at `src/assets/images/thegrove_logo.jpg` is the cornerstone of our visual identity. It represents the target quality and feel for the game's visuals.

### Color Palette

The color palette should be drawn from the logo, featuring deep, earthy tones, bioluminescent glows, and a sense of mystery.

### UI/UX

The user interface should be minimal and intuitive, so as not to detract from the immersive experience. It should feel like a natural extension of the game world.
